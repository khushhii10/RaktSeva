About Us

Rakt Seva is more than just a platform; it’s a community-driven mission to connect those in life-critical situations with people willing to offer the gift of blood. In moments of urgent need, finding a blood donor swiftly can be a matter of life and death. We believe no one should face such a moment alone, and that’s why Rakt Seva was created—to ensure that help is always just a click away. Our platform brings together hospitals, communities, and individuals in need of blood with compassionate donors ready to make a difference.
